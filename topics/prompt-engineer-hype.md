## What is Prompt Engineer Hype?

Prompt Engineer Hype is the belief that 'prompt engineering' is a durable career path or skill moat — when in reality, AI systems are rapidly making prompting unnecessary.

## Start Here

Prompt engineering was a temporary skill gap, not a career. As AI models improve, they need less precise prompting. The people who built careers around 'talking to AI correctly' will find that skill commoditized within months. The real moat is judgment: knowing what to ask, evaluating whether the output is right, and owning the decision that follows.
