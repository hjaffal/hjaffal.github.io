## What is The Collapse of Busy Work?

The Collapse of Busy Work describes how AI is eliminating the routine tasks that filled most knowledge workers' days — leaving only judgment work, which many professionals aren't prepared for.

## Start Here

Most professionals spend 60-80% of their time on tasks that AI can now handle: formatting reports, scheduling, summarizing meetings, pulling data, routing requests. When that work disappears, what's left? If the answer is 'not much,' that's the exposure. The professionals who thrive are the ones whose remaining 20-40% is pure judgment.
