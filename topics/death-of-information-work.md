## What is The Death of Information Work?

The Death of Information Work describes the rapid automation of roles built entirely around moving, formatting, summarizing, and routing information between systems or people.

## Start Here

If your job can be described as 'I take information from here and put it there in a different format,' you're in the most exposed category. AI doesn't just do this faster — it eliminates the need for a human in the loop entirely. The path forward is moving from information processing to decision ownership.
