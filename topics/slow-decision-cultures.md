## What is Slow Decision Cultures?

Slow Decision Cultures are organizations where approval chains, meeting dependencies, and unclear ownership prevent timely action — even when signals are clear and urgent.

## Start Here

Most organizations detect problems faster than they can decide what to do about them. AI makes this worse: it surfaces signals in milliseconds, but the approval process still takes days. The result is a growing gap between detection speed and decision speed. If your team needs a meeting to act on a risk signal, you have a slow decision culture.
