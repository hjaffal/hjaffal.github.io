## What is Intelligence vs Reporting?

Intelligence vs Reporting is the fundamental distinction between explaining what happened (reporting) and changing what happens next (intelligence). Most organizations confuse the two.

## Start Here

If your metrics don't trigger action, they're decoration. Reporting tells you what the number was. Intelligence tells you what to do about it, who should act, and how fast. The difference isn't sophistication — it's whether the output connects to a decision.
