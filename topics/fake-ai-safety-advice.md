## What is Fake AI Safety Advice?

Fake AI Safety Advice is the generic career guidance that gives professionals false comfort about their exposure to AI — 'just learn prompt engineering' or 'soft skills will save you.'

## Start Here

Most AI career advice is dangerously generic. It tells people to 'upskill' without specifying what skills actually matter. It claims 'creativity' and 'empathy' are safe without acknowledging that AI is already handling creative and empathetic tasks. The real question isn't whether AI will affect your job — it's which specific tasks in your role are exposed and what you should do about it this month.
