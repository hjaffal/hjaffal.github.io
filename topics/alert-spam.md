## What is Alert Spam?

Alert Spam is when organizations generate so many notifications, warnings, and signals that real threats get buried in noise — leading to alert fatigue and missed critical events.

## Start Here

More alerts don't mean better detection. They mean more noise. When everything is urgent, nothing is. The teams that catch real threats aren't the ones with the most alerts — they're the ones with the fewest, most meaningful signals connected to immediate action authority.
