## What is White-Collar Automation?

White-Collar Automation is the replacement of knowledge work tasks — reporting, summarizing, routing, formatting — by AI systems that do them faster, cheaper, and at scale.

## Start Here

AI isn't coming for blue-collar jobs first. It's coming for the middle layer of knowledge work: the analysts who pull data, the coordinators who route information, the report builders who summarize what happened. These tasks are repetitive, rules-based, and disconnected from judgment — exactly what AI handles best.
