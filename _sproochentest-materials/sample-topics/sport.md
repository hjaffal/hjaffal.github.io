1. Maacht Dir gär Sport an Ärer Fräizäit? — *Do you like doing sport in your free time?*
2. Firwat maacht Dir Sport? Wat ass Är Motivatioun? — *Why do you do sport? What is your motivation?*
3. Wat fir Kleeder hutt Dir u fir Sport ze maachen? — *What clothes do you wear for sport?*
4. Sidd Dir an engem Sportsveräin? — *Are you in a sports club?*
5. Maacht Dir Sport doheem? — *Do you do sport at home?*
6. Maacht Dir léiwer Sport eleng oder an engem Club? — *Do you prefer sport alone or in a club?*
7. Wat sinn d'Virdeeler vun engem Gruppesport? — *What are the advantages of group sport?*
8. Hutt Dir Sport an Ärer Kandheet gemaach? — *Did you do sport in your childhood?*
9. Kuckt Dir Sport op der Tëlee? — *Do you watch sport on TV?*
10. Wat fir e Sport ass populär an Ärem Heemechtsland? — *What sport is popular in your home country?*
11. Kennt Dir e lëtzebuergesche Sportler? — *Do you know a Luxembourgish athlete?*
12. Ass Sport an der Schoul wichteg? — *Is sport in school important?*
