1. Wéini schenkt Dir enger Persoun e Kaddo? — *When do you give someone a gift?*
2. Schenkt Dir léiwer perséinlech oder onperséinlech Kaddoen? — *Do you prefer giving personal or impersonal gifts?*
3. Wat schenkt Dir Ärem Partner / Äre Kanner / Ärer Famill? — *What do you give your partner/children/family?*
4. Wou kaaft Dir Kaddoen? Firwat? — *Where do you buy gifts? Why?*
5. Wat ass fir Iech en ideale Kaddo? — *What is an ideal gift for you?*
6. Wat mengt Dir, muss e Kaddo deier sinn? — *Do you think a gift has to be expensive?*
7. Wéini hutt Dir fir d'lescht e Kaddo kritt? Vu wiem? — *When did you last receive a gift? From whom?*
8. Wat war dee schéinste Kaddo, deen Dir kritt hutt? — *What was the nicest gift you received?*
9. Wéi reagéiert Dir, wann Dir e Kaddo kritt, deen Iech net gefält? — *How do you react when you get a gift you don't like?*
10. Wéini schenkt Dir enger Persoun eppes an Ärem Heemechtsland? — *When do you give gifts in your home country?*
11. Wat schenkt Dir engem Kolleeg, dee 60 Joer kritt? — *What do you give a colleague turning 60?*
12. Dir sidd op en Owesiessen bei Frënn invitéiert. Wat bréngt Dir mat? — *You're invited to dinner at friends'. What do you bring?*
