1. Wéi wichteg ass d'Moud fir Iech? — *How important is fashion for you?*
2. Wéi informéiert Dir Iech iwwer d'Moud? — *How do you inform yourself about fashion?*
3. Wéi dacks gitt Dir Kleeder akafen? — *How often do you go clothes shopping?*
4. Mat wiem gitt Dir Kleeder akafen? — *With whom do you go clothes shopping?*
5. Wou kaaft Dir d'Kleeder? — *Where do you buy clothes?*
6. Kaaft Dir och Kleeder am Ausland? — *Do you also buy clothes abroad?*
7. Kaaft Dir Kleeder op Internet? Wat sinn d'Virdeeler? — *Do you buy clothes online? What are the advantages?*
8. Wat fir Kleeder dot Dir un fir schaffen ze goen? — *What clothes do you wear to go to work?*
9. Gitt Dir an d'Solden? — *Do you go to the sales?*
10. Kaaft Dir Occasiounskleeder? — *Do you buy second-hand clothes?*
11. Dot Dir léiwer sportlech oder klassesch Kleeder un? — *Do you prefer sporty or classic clothes?*
12. Mussen d'Kanner an Ärem Heemechtsland eng Uniform unhunn? — *Do children in your home country have to wear a uniform?*
13. Wat fir Accessoiren hutt Dir all Dag un? — *What accessories do you wear every day?*
