Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

You fill in details about your role and tasks. Our AI analyzes your profile against current automation trends and sends you a personalized report by email.

Is my data stored?

Your form inputs are sent to generate the report and are not stored after the email is sent. Your email is added to the newsletter if you consent.

How accurate is the risk score?

The score is an estimate based on current AI capabilities and trends. It is not a prediction. Use it as a starting point for planning, not as a certainty.

How long does it take?

The form takes about 3 minutes. Your report arrives by email within 1–2 minutes after submission.
