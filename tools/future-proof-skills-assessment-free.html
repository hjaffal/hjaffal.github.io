What are future-proof skills?

Skills that stay valuable as AI automates routine work: judgment, communication, business understanding, problem-solving, and AI literacy.

Can AI replace my job?

AI replaces tasks before it replaces jobs. People who only execute repeatable tasks are more exposed. People who use judgment and influence decisions are harder to replace.
