How AI Is Changing Jobs

AI is not coming for your job in one move. It is coming for your tasks — one at a time. Reporting, data entry, scheduling, first-draft writing, pattern matching, and routine analysis are already being automated. The professionals who only do those tasks are the most exposed.

But AI cannot replace judgment under uncertainty, stakeholder navigation, cross-domain thinking, or accountability for outcomes. The question is not whether AI will affect your role. The question is which parts of your role are at risk — and whether you are building the skills that survive.
