1. Wéi oft gitt Dir an d'Vakanz? — *How often do you go on holiday?*
2. Gitt Dir eleng oder mat anere Persounen an d'Vakanz? — *Do you go on holiday alone or with others?*
3. Wuer gitt Dir gewéinlech an d'Vakanz? — *Where do you usually go on holiday?*
4. Maacht Dir léiwer kuerz oder laang Vakanzen? — *Do you prefer short or long holidays?*
5. Wéi bucht Dir Är Vakanzen? — *How do you book your holidays?*
6. Wat sinn d'Virdeeler fir online ze buchen? — *What are the advantages of booking online?*
7. Wat sinn Är Krittäre fir en Hotel ze buchen? — *What are your criteria for booking a hotel?*
8. Lount Dir eng Wunneng fir d'Vakanz? — *Do you rent an apartment for holidays?*
9. Gitt Dir op de Camping? — *Do you go camping?*
10. Hutt Dir schonn eng Croisière gemaach? — *Have you ever done a cruise?*
11. Wat fir Aktivitéite maacht Dir an der Vakanz? — *What activities do you do on holiday?*
12. Wat ass Är Inspiratioun fir ze reesen? — *What is your inspiration for travelling?*
13. Gitt Dir an de Wantersport? — *Do you go winter sports?*
14. Wou waart Dir fir d'lescht an der Vakanz? — *Where were you last on holiday?*
15. Wat sinn Är Vakanzpläng fir den nächste Summer? — *What are your holiday plans for next summer?*
