1. Wéi oft maacht Dir d'Kommissiounen? — *How often do you do the shopping?*
2. Wou maacht Dir d'Kommissiounen? Mat wiem? — *Where do you shop? With whom?*
3. Wéi gitt Dir normalerweis akafen? — *How do you normally go shopping?*
4. Wat hutt Dir haut kaaft? Wou? — *What did you buy today? Where?*
5. Wat kaaft Dir am Supermarché? — *What do you buy at the supermarket?*
6. Maacht Dir gär d'Kommissiounen? — *Do you like doing the shopping?*
7. Kaaft Dir och Bioprodukter? — *Do you also buy organic products?*
8. Gitt Dir heiansdo op de Maart akafen? — *Do you sometimes go to the market?*
9. Kaaft Dir Liewensmëttelen online? — *Do you buy groceries online?*
10. Wéini hutt Dir fir d'lescht e Kaddo kaaft? — *When did you last buy a gift?*
11. Kaaft Dir Saachen um Fluchhafen? — *Do you buy things at the airport?*
12. Ass et einfach Produkter aus Ärem Heemechtsland ze fannen? — *Is it easy to find products from your home country?*
13. Wou kaaft Dir Wäsch- a Botzmëttelen? — *Where do you buy cleaning products?*
14. Wou hutt Dir fir d'lescht Kleeder kaaft? — *Where did you last buy clothes?*
15. Wéi sinn d'Ëffnungszäiten vun de Supermarchéen an Ärem Heemechtsland? — *What are supermarket opening hours in your home country?*
16. Wann Dir misst e Buttek opmaachen, wat géift Dir verkafen? — *If you had to open a shop, what would you sell?*
