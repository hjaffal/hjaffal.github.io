1. Gesond liewen: wat heescht dat fir Iech? — *Healthy living: what does that mean for you?*
2. Wat maacht Dir fir gesond ze liewen? — *What do you do to live healthily?*
3. Wat iesst an drénkt Dir fir gesond ze bleiwen? — *What do you eat and drink to stay healthy?*
4. Iesst Dir Fastfood? Ass dat ongesond? — *Do you eat fast food? Is that unhealthy?*
5. Wat mengt Dir: Ass vegetaresch / vegan iessen gesond? — *What do you think: is vegetarian/vegan eating healthy?*
6. Iesst Dir Bioprodukter? Ass et méi gesond Bioprodukter ze iessen? — *Do you eat organic products? Is it healthier to eat organic?*
7. Ass de Schlof wichteg fir Iech? — *Is sleep important for you?*
8. Wéi informéiert Dir Iech iwwer d'Gesondheet? — *How do you inform yourself about health?*
9. Hutt Dir Hausdéieren? Ass dat gutt fir d'Gesondheet? — *Do you have pets? Is that good for health?*
10. Wat mengt Dir: sinn Zigaretten an Alkohol ongesond? — *What do you think: are cigarettes and alcohol unhealthy?*
11. Dir sidd Dokter, wat géift Dir mir recommandéiere fir gesond ze liewen? — *You are a doctor, what would you recommend to live healthily?*
12. Ass vill Sport maachen gutt fir d'Gesondheet? — *Is doing a lot of sport good for health?*
13. Wat maacht Dir, wann Dir Féiwer / Kappwéi hutt? — *What do you do when you have a fever/headache?*
