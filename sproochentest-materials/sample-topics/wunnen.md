1. Wou wunnt Dir? Säit wéini? — *Where do you live? Since when?*
2. Wou hutt Dir virdru gewunnt? — *Where did you live before?*
3. Beschreift Är Wunneng! — *Describe your apartment!*
4. Wat ass déi dominant Faarf an Ärer Wunneng? — *What is the dominant colour in your apartment?*
5. Wou verbréngt Dir vill Zäit doheem? — *Where do you spend a lot of time at home?*
6. Ass et einfach eng Parkplaz ze fannen? — *Is it easy to find a parking spot?*
7. Ass Äre Quartier geféierlech oder sécher? — *Is your neighbourhood dangerous or safe?*
8. Gëtt et vill Trafic an Ärem Quartier? — *Is there a lot of traffic in your neighbourhood?*
9. Wat fir Butteker ginn et do? — *What shops are there?*
10. Kënnt Dir e Café / Restaurant do recommandéieren? — *Can you recommend a café/restaurant there?*
11. Wat kann ee mat Kanner an Ärem Quartier maachen? — *What can you do with children in your neighbourhood?*
12. Wéi gefält Iech Äre Quartier? Firwat? — *How do you like your neighbourhood? Why?*
13. Wat gefält Iech net esou gutt do? — *What don't you like so much there?*
14. Wéi sinn d'Verbindunge vun Ärem Wunnuert op d'Aarbecht? — *How are the connections from your home to work?*
15. Sinn d'Hausdéieren an der Residence erlaabt? — *Are pets allowed in the building?*
16. Gëtt et do och e Veräin oder e Club? — *Is there also a club or association there?*
17. Kennt Dir Är Noperen? Wéi si si? — *Do you know your neighbours? What are they like?*
18. Wunnt Dir léiwer an enger Stad oder an engem Duerf? — *Do you prefer living in a city or a village?*
