1. Lauschtert Dir gär Musek? — *Do you like listening to music?*
2. Wéi eng Musek lauschtert Dir am léifsten? — *What music do you like listening to most?*
3. Spillt Dir e Museksinstrument? — *Do you play a musical instrument?*
4. Spillt een an Ärer Famill en Instrument? — *Does anyone in your family play an instrument?*
5. Hutt Dir an der Schoul sangen geléiert? — *Did you learn to sing in school?*
6. Hutt Dir als Kand gär Musek gelauschtert? — *Did you like listening to music as a child?*
7. Gitt Dir gären op Concerten? — *Do you like going to concerts?*
8. Wou lauschtert Dir meeschtens Musek? — *Where do you mostly listen to music?*
9. Wéini lauschtert Dir gäre Musek? — *When do you like listening to music?*
10. Hutt Dir eng Liblingsband oder e Lieblingssänger? — *Do you have a favourite band or singer?*
11. Kennt Dir e berühmte Museker aus Lëtzebuerg? — *Do you know a famous musician from Luxembourg?*
12. Wéi fillt Dir Iech, wann Dir Musek lauschtert? — *How do you feel when you listen to music?*
13. Wat fir eng App benotzt Dir fir Musek ze lauschteren? — *What app do you use to listen to music?*
14. Wéi eng traditionell Musek ginn et an Ärem Heemechtsland? — *What traditional music exists in your home country?*
