1. Wat maacht Dir gär an der Woch no der Aarbecht? — *What do you like doing after work during the week?*
2. Wat maacht Dir gär de Weekend? Mat wiem? — *What do you like doing on weekends? With whom?*
3. Wat sinn Är Hobbyen doheem? — *What are your hobbies at home?*
4. Sidd Dir Member an engem Veräin / Club? — *Are you a member of a club/association?*
5. Sidd Dir kreativ an Ärer Fräizäit? — *Are you creative in your free time?*
6. Wat fir kreativ Aktivitéite kann een an der Fräizäit maachen? — *What creative activities can you do in your free time?*
7. Wéi kann ee Kanner motivéieren, méi kreativ ze sinn? — *How can you motivate children to be more creative?*
8. Kann ee Kreativitéit léieren? — *Can you learn creativity?*
9. Wat fir en Hobby hutt Dir an Ärer Kandheet gemaach? — *What hobby did you have in your childhood?*
10. Waren d'Leit fréier méi kreativ wéi haut? — *Were people more creative in the past than today?*
