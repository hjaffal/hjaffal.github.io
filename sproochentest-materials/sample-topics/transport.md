1. Vu wou kommt Dir elo? — *Where are you coming from now?*
2. Wéi sidd Dir haut an den INL komm? — *How did you get to INL today?*
3. Hutt Dir en Auto? Beschreift Ären Auto. — *Do you have a car? Describe your car.*
4. Wéi gitt Dir op d'Aarbecht? — *How do you get to work?*
5. Wat fir Transportmëttele benotzt Dir fir an d'Vakanz ze goen? — *What transport do you use to go on holiday?*
6. Wat fir Transportmëttele benotzt Dir an Ärer Fräizäit? — *What transport do you use in your free time?*
7. Benotzt Dir de "bloe Vëlo" oder e Vëlo vun Ärer Gemeng? — *Do you use the "blue bike" or a bike from your municipality?*
8. Wat fir Transportmëttelen hëlt Dir nach? Wéini? — *What other transport do you take? When?*
9. Benotzt Dir méi oft den ëffentlechen Transport säit en gratis ass? — *Do you use public transport more since it's free?*
10. Wat fir Virdeeler huet de Fliger / den Tram? — *What advantages does the plane/tram have?*
11. Gitt Dir ze Fouss? Wéini? Wou? — *Do you walk? When? Where?*
