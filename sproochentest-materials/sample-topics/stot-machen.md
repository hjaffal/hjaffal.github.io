1. Wunnt Dir eleng? — *Do you live alone?*
2. Wien hëlleft Iech de Stot ze maachen? — *Who helps you do the housework?*
3. Wéini maacht Dir de Stot? — *When do you do the housework?*
4. Wat maacht Dir am Stot? — *What do you do for housework?*
5. Wat hutt Dir haut am Stot gemaach? — *What housework did you do today?*
6. Wat mécht Äre Partner / Mann / Är Kanner? — *What does your partner / husband / your children do?*
7. Hutt Dir eng Botzfra? Wat mécht si? — *Do you have a cleaning lady? What does she do?*
8. Maacht Dir gär de Stot? Firwat? Firwat net? — *Do you like doing housework? Why? Why not?*
9. Hutt Dir eng Spullmaschinn? Wat ass de Virdeel? — *Do you have a dishwasher? What is the advantage?*
10. Wien raumt d'Spullmaschinn an an aus? — *Who loads and unloads the dishwasher?*
11. Wéini hutt Dir fir d'lescht eng Wäsch gemaach? — *When did you last do laundry?*
12. Streckt Dir? Streckt Dir gär? Wat maacht Dir wann Dir streckt? — *Do you iron? Do you like ironing? What do you do when you iron?*
13. Wien ass responsabel fir ze kachen? — *Who is responsible for cooking?*
14. Hutt Dir haut gekacht? — *Did you cook today?*
15. Wie mécht d'Poubelle eidel? — *Who empties the bin?*
