1. Liest Dir gär? Wa jo, wat liest Dir? — *Do you like reading? If yes, what do you read?*
2. Wat hutt Dir haut gelies? — *What did you read today?*
3. Hutt Dir an Ärer Kandheet gelies? — *Did you read in your childhood?*
4. Wat fir Bicher hutt Dir als Kand gäre gelies? — *What books did you like reading as a child?*
5. Wat fir Bicher hutt Dir an der Schoul gelies? — *What books did you read in school?*
6. Kënnt Dir e Buch fir Kanner recommandéieren? — *Can you recommend a book for children?*
7. A wéi enger Sprooch liest Dir? — *In which language do you read?*
8. Kënnt Dir e Buch fir Lëtzebuergesch ze léieren recommandéieren? — *Can you recommend a book for learning Luxembourgish?*
9. Wéini liest Dir? — *When do you read?*
10. Wou liest Dir gär doheem? — *Where do you like reading at home?*
11. Gitt Dir heiansdo an d'Bibliothéik? — *Do you sometimes go to the library?*
12. Wou kaaft Dir d'Bicher? — *Where do you buy books?*
13. Wat liest Dir ausser Bicher? — *What do you read besides books?*
14. Liest Dir och digital Bicher? — *Do you also read digital books?*
15. Wat sinn d'Virdeeler an d'Nodeeler vum Kindle? — *What are the advantages and disadvantages of Kindle?*
16. Wat liest Dir am Moment? — *What are you reading at the moment?*
17. Wéini hutt Dir fir d'lescht e Buch gelies? — *When did you last read a book?*
18. Hutt Dir e Liblingsauteur? — *Do you have a favourite author?*
19. Liest Dir och d'Zeitung? Online oder Pabeierversioun? — *Do you also read the newspaper? Online or paper?*
20. Wat fir eng Sektioun(en) liest Dir an enger Zeitung? — *What sections do you read in a newspaper?*
21. Wat sinn d'Virdeeler vun der Pabeierversioun? — *What are the advantages of the paper version?*
22. Wat maacht Dir léiwer: Tëlee kucken oder liesen? — *What do you prefer: watching TV or reading?*
